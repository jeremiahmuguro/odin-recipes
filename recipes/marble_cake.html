Marble Cake

Description

Makes 3 servings

Preparation time: 2hrs

Ingredients
  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Eggs - 3
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 1/4 cup
  • Cocoa - 3 tbsp
  • Vanilla - 1 tbsp
  • Butter - 3 scoops

Steps
  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. Add the wet ingredients gradually while mixing
  3. Prep the baking tin by spreading butter and sprinkling some flour
  4. Preheat the oven at 180 deg for 10 min
  5. Place the batter inside the oven and bake for 1hour at 200 deg
  6. Place the cake in a rack to cool
  7. Cut a slice and enjoy your cake
  8. Serve with yoghurt or apple juice
